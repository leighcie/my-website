Mintrak®2 — Rebuilt a Mission-Critical SaaS Platform in 5 Months and Turned It Into a Product Users Bragged About

Product Strategy & Vision Execution & Technical Delivery Business Impact & Market Growth

Executive Summary

When Titan Lenders Corp lost access to its outsourced reconciliation platform (Mintrak), we had 6 months to rebuild or risk losing 30 mortgage-servicer customers in a highly regulated MERS compliance market.

Instead of cloning the old system, I interviewed all customers and uncovered the deeper issue: the tool wasn't part of their workflow, created constant file-format headaches, and required IT involvement for even basic changes.

The vision: A reconciliation tool anyone could use without IT, that accepted any file format, showed users exactly what was happening, and integrated naturally into monthly compliance workflows.

Outcomes

  • Launched MVP in 5 months, migrated all customers with 0 churn
  • Scaled to 160+ customers and ~$5M ARR
  • Became a market leader; users publicly endorsed it at MERS conferences
  • Established trusted relationships with MERS, who validated our comparison logic over their internal tools

The Real Problem

Although the urgent risk was losing platform access, discovery showed the deeper challenges:

  • Low technical accessibility: rigid file formats, error-prone uploads, IT dependency
  • Zero transparency: users couldn't see what caused discrepancies or what they'd fixed
  • No learning curve: many repeated the same issues monthly
  • Industry backwardness: competitors required strict formats and took weeks to return results

Insight: Customers didn't just need reconciliation — they needed flexibility, visibility, and control.

Product Vision

North Star: Flexible data in, transparent logic out, with self-service tools that remove IT from the equation.

Three principles

  1. Flexibility over rigidity: accept the data users can provide
  2. Transparency over black box: show comparison logic and trends
  3. Self-service over dependency: empower users with mapping + resolution tools

What We Built (MVP → Scale)

MVP (5 months)

  • Support for core formats + discrepancy reporting
  • Self-service upload
  • Dashboard showing current & historical issues
  • Seamless migration of all existing customer mappings

Post-MVP

  • Custom file-mapping tool: "Bring any file format" became our #1 differentiator
  • Trend analysis, resolution tracking, and MERS-formatted export
  • Faster, clearer reconciliation workflows that reduced time from weeks to minutes/hours

Execution Under Constraints

  • 2–3 outsourced devs, me handling product, UX, QA, and customer enablement
  • MVP built in 5 months, training + migration in month 6
  • Chose flexible JSON model to move fast, with a deliberate plan to refactor later
  • Sales enablement (training, one-pagers, bundled pricing) unlocked cross-sell to 200+ new customers across related MERS services

Impact

Business

  • From 30 → 160+ customers
  • ~$5M ARR with minimal operational overhead
  • Created scalable, product-led growth in a compliance-heavy market

Customer

  • Reconciliation dropped from weeks → minutes
  • Users gained visibility and control competitors couldn't match
"We use Mintrak®2 and I don't know why anyone would use anything else."
— Customer statement at a MERS conference

Industry

  • Built strong relationships with MERS; they validated our comparison logic and used our outputs to refine their own audit findings

Why This Matters

Mintrak®2 became a market-defining product because we treated a crisis as an opportunity to rethink the experience from scratch. Deep discovery + a strong vision + tangible execution turned a forced rebuild into a product customers loved — and advocated for publicly.
