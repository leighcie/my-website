Case Studies

Overview

I spearheaded the overhaul of the renewals process for the state insurance marketplace to enhance efficiency and user experience.

Problem Statement

The renewals process required manual initiation and continuous monitoring, leading to substantial system downtimes—typically exceeding 8 calendar days.

Solution

We mapped out existing processes and pain points, then evaluated our newly designed service-oriented architecture for enhancements. This included conducting detailed analysis, mapping workflows, designing automated solutions, and implementing the new process in an AWS environment.

Results

The new automated process successfully processed 87% of renewals within 24 hours, surpassing our goal to process 80% of renewals within 3 days.

Key Learnings

Success relies on shared ownership across teams. The process involved multiple teams, requiring intensive collaboration under tight deadlines. Effective collaboration, documentation of edge cases, and streamlined error handling were key to success.

Overview

I led the enhancement of Mintrak2, a SaaS platform used by servicers and sub-servicers in the mortgage industry, to improve efficiency and user satisfaction in data reconciliation with the MERS registry.

Problem Statement

Customers frequently encountered the same discrepancies each month due to delayed system updates, making the reconciliation process inefficient and frustrating.

Solution

We analyzed existing processes and customer feedback, then implemented targeted improvements including discrepancy sorting for prioritization and a custom export feature for IT teams.

Results

The new features led to significantly happier clients and a 60% reduction in recurring discrepancies within 3 months, exceeding our target of 50% reduction within 6 months.

Key Learnings

Detailed and iterative testing is essential, especially when dealing with custom file types. Effective communication and collaboration across teams ensured alignment, while keeping the design intuitive was critical for user adoption.
